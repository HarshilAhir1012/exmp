Rotaract Club of NLJIET

    Our Vision

    To inspire and empower young leaders to create a lasting positive impact in our community and the world through evolution, impact, and community service.

    Our Mission

    To provide opportunities for personal and professional development, foster fellowship, and serve society through impactful projects and initiatives that translate inspiration into tangible, real-world change.

    Our Theme

    The theme for the Rotaract year 2025-26 is "Inspire to Impact." This theme represents an evolution from previous years of leading and engaging in collaborations, with a sharpened focus on ensuring that inspiration translates into tangible, real-world change. The overarching goal for the year is to "Evolve," embracing new challenges and making a lasting difference.

    About Our Club

    The Rotaract Club of NLJ Institute of Engineering & Technology, Ahmedabad, India, has commenced the new Rotaract year 2025-26 with a clear vision of evolution, impact, and community service. Under new leadership, the club has already initiated impactful projects and outlined its theme for the year ahead.
